The state of Chihuahua is one of the 31 states of Mexico , and the largest; it is located in the northwestern part of the country. It has a mainland area of 244,938 km². It is largely a desert state, although there are areas of significant rainfall and green forests. In fact, Chihuahua state has more forest than any other Mexican state.
