Cane Corso

The Cane Corso (pronounced kah-neh kor-so [ˈkaːne ˈkɔrso] ) from Italian cane (dog) and "corso" from the Latin "Cohors" meaning "protector", also known as the Italian Mastiff , [ citation needed ] is a large Italian breed of dog , for years valued highly in Italy as a companion and guard dog . [2]
