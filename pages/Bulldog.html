Bulldog

The Bulldog , also known as the English Bulldog or British Bulldog , is a medium-sized breed of dog . It is a muscular, hefty dog with a wrinkled face and a distinctive pushed-in nose. [4] The American Kennel Club (AKC), The Kennel Club (UK), and the United Kennel Club (UKC) oversee breeding records. Bulldogs were the fourth most popular purebred in the US in 2016 according to the American Kennel Club. [5]
