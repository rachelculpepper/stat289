The Staffordshire Bull Terrier is an old time breed of dog , originally bred for bull-baiting, lion fighting, and eventually dog fighting. They originated in the "Black Country" of England . The Staffordshire Bull Terrier evolved out of a "Proto-Stafford" that was originally bred for the beforementioned sports. At the time that they were being developed there were no written standards for their physical composition. In the early part of the 20th century they gained respectablity and was accepted into the Kennel Club of England as the Staffordshire Bull Terrier - not to be confused with the White Bull Terrier. The Staffordshire Bull Terrier is an English breed of dog and should not be confused with their larger cousin the American Staffordshire Terrier or the American Pit Bull Terrrier.
