The 50 Most Popular Dog Breeds Worldwide

These pages look at the characteristics of the Wikipedia pages for the top 50 dog breeds worldwide. The ranking of the most popular dog breeds was taken from a combination of the 2013 list of most popular dog breeds on the Wikipedia page listing the most popular dog breeds, which lists the most popular 30 breeds. The remaining breeds came from this page listing the 50 most popular breeds worldwide.

Some of the characteristics explored in these pages include the page histories since they were first created, the languages into which each page has been translated, a measure of how often a given page is linked to by the other pages in the set, and some description page length, included images, etc.
